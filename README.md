## 🔍 2. Deep-Dive Insights (Analytical Findings)

* **Customer Profiling:** The majority of high-value customers are in the "Graduation" and "PhD" segments, showing a direct link between education level and total spending.
* **Family Impact:** Customers with "No Children" or "1 Child" tend to have a higher average purchase value compared to larger households.
* **Income Correlation:** A strong positive correlation exists between Annual Income and spending on luxury items like Meat and Gold.
* **Channel Preference:** While Web purchases are high, "Store Purchases" still dominate the final transaction volume, suggesting a need for a seamless Omni-channel experience.

---

## 💡 3. Strategic Recommendations

1.  **Targeted Promotion:** Launch premium loyalty programs specifically for PhD and Masters-level customers, as they show the highest spending potential.
2.  **Web Channel Optimization:** Implement "Online Exclusive" offers to convert more store shoppers into web shoppers, reducing operational costs.
3.  **Retention Strategy:** Focus on customers who accepted "Campaign 1" and "Campaign 5", as they show the highest retention potential.
4.  **Family-Centric Deals:** Introduce bundled discounts for families with children to increase their average basket size in the supermarket.
